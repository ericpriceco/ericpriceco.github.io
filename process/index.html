GET TO KNOW MY PROCESS

Overview of the process I will use on your next project

Research / Planning

During the research phase I will ask a lot questions to get an idea about your business, competition, goals and customers. This information will greatly help shape your site. I will create a site map of the site with a navigation layout. I will always keep you updated on the progress of these stages.

Design

After a spec sheet for the site is agreed on, I will start sketching designs that I will turn into digital wireframes for review. Wireframes are barebones layout designs without content, typography or color. The next step is a site mockup that will resemble the final site before the coding process begins.

Develop / Testing

Once the final site mockup is approved, I will begin the coding process to make the site usable and conforms to the latest industry standards. I will run cross-browser tests and layout tests on different screens such as phones and tablets. I will post the site to a test environment for review.

Launch

When the site has gone through its final polishing after review of the test environment, I will publish the site to the provided host environment and monitor the google analytics.

Post-Launch

After publishing the site to the production environment, I will write up documentation on your site with tools used, login credentials and a site map. I will also send a soft-copy of all source files and assets used on the site.

Support

Provide two weeks of support for any minor changes or bug fixes found after launching the site and on-going maintenance is available if needed. If a content management system was added in the project, I can provide training for making changes through the interface.

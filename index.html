RESPONSIVE DESIGN

I create responsive designs that allow websites to adjust to a variety of screen sizes and devices. With mobile use projected to surpass PC, it's vital to have an easy to navigate mobile website. No visitor will have anything less than a perfect experience on whatever screen-size they use.

UX and UI Design

First impressions are everything and you only have a few seconds to capture a visitors attention before they move on. My hand-coded sites focus on clean, modern designs with clear visibility of your content and brand, while providing easy navigation for your visitors.

CMS INTERGRATION

Provide my clients with a CMS that gives them control to manage content after the sites launch. I use the Perch CMS to handle this. The simple to use interface requires no HTML experience and with its light-weight design, it provides quicker response time.

ABOUT

I’m Eric Price, a freelance web designer and developer in the Raleigh area of North Carolina, specializing in responsive web design. I have been coding both personally and professionally for years and the last few years have been focused primarily on web development.

I have a clean design style where content works with the overall design, not against it. That style also applies to my coding, with clean code that is optimized for performance. My design process from the beginning accounts for all screen sizes, so it's not necessary to remove or adjust content to fit a mobile screen.

I mainly work with small companies, individuals and startups and take pride in the relationships I build with my clients. This is built through communication, dependability and providing value for their business. Get in touch with me if you would like to discuss a project with me.

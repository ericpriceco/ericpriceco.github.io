ABOUT ME

Freelance web developer and designer

I’m Eric Price, a freelance web designer and developer in the Raleigh area of North Carolina, specializing in responsive web design and front-end development. I have been coding both personally and professionally for years and the last few years have been focused primarily on web development. I have a real passion for creating websites and believe a website should be easily accessible to any visitor on any screen whether that is a phone or a large monitor.

I have a minimalistic and clean design style where content works with the overall design, not against it. That style also applies to my coding, with clean code that is optimized for performance and search engines. My design process from the beginning accounts for all screen sizes. With this approach, it’s not necessary to remove content to fit a mobile screen.

I mainly work with small companies, individuals and startups and take pride in the relationships I build with my clients. This is built through communication, dependability and providing value for their business. Please get in touch if you would like to discuss a project with me.

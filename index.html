Hi, I'm Eric Price, a freelance web developer and designer based out of the Raleigh area in North Carolina. I have a passion for creating clean, modern responsive websites that makes your content and brand stand out.

I believe a website should be easily accessible to any visitor on any screen whether that is a phone or a large monitor. My websites are hand-coded line by line because when a website is coded well, it works faster and more efficiently.

Responsive Design

Make it easy for your mobile customers to navigate your website. I create responsive designs that allow your website to adjust to a variety of screen sizes whether it's a phone, tablet or large monitor.

CMS

Content Management Systems allow you to edit parts of your website from an easy to use interface. I believe giving people the ability to make content changes without relying on someone else to make it for them.

UX and UI Design

My hand-coded sites focus on clean designs with clear visibility of your content and brand. Your handcrafted website is developed using standard compliant coding and tested for cross-browser compatibility.

Maintenance

Ongoing maintenance is available if needed giving you peace of mind. If you need help managing new content for your site or need a quick change, I'm just a phone call away.

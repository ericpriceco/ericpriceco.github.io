LEARN ABOUT MY SERVICES

Smart Design

Research has shown that you only have a few seconds to capture someone’s attention before they decide to move on or stay on your site. This is why making a first good impression is key to customer conversion. There are two major factors in preventing users from abandoning your site: page load times and content that quickly grabs the attention of your visitor.

My clean designs will make your most relevant information stand out and highlight your brand value, while allowing your visitors to easily navigate your site.

Hand-Coded

My site optimization techniques factor in viewers on slow mobile networks to fast broadband connections by serving specific content based off of screen size. A lot of web designers grab a Wordpress template and modify the content for their client.

This approach can lead to boring, uninspired designs with slow page load times due to bloated unnecessary code. My designs are hand-coded with response time always in mind and developed using industry standard compliant coding that is cross-browser tested.

Responsive Web Design

When viewing your website on a large screen, laptop, mobile or tablet device, your content will always look good. Mobile browsing is increasing every year and it’s projected to surpass PC browsing this year. Making sure your website is easy to navigate on mobile screens is vital to keeping visitors on your site.

A responsive website automatically knows what screen size it is being viewed on and adapts by changing and re-ordering the layout for optimal viewing. No visitor will have anything less than a perfect experience on whatever screen-size they use.

Content Management System

A Content Management System (or CMS for short) allows you to edit parts of your website from a simple user-interface; making sure your website reflects your changing brand. I can integrate a content management system for you that requires no knowledge of HTML to edit content on your pages or even manage a blog. I use a CMS that is light-weight and will not increase page load time for your visitors.
